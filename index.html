About us

Our Mission is to provide high quality of education in safe, secure and nurturing environment to develop our students into progressive thinkers and lifelong bearers of skills that will prepare them for challenges of a dynamically changing world.

Our Aim and our Goal is to nurture persons who are intellectually competent as well as humanely compassionate, who understand the society they live in and feel responsible for its transformation.

To inculcate habit in students to respect for each person's dignity and thereby to promote equality of opportunity and social justice.

To enable students to realize that persons are interdependent, and hence the benefits of education they receive have to be placed at the service of the privileged, the poor, the oppressed and the marginalized irrespective of cast, class, and creed.

To give Quality education for the students, with best ever infrastructure
